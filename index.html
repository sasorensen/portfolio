About

Stephanie has spent her life using her hands to create a life she loves as well as years of experience working with children. She has a husband and three children of her own whom she loves. Her kids ages range from 13 to 18 years old. Stephanie has a Fine Arts Bachelor's degree from San Diego State University in Applied Design with an emphasis in Fiber/Textiles. She loves to design functional pieces such as bags, quilts, jewelry, and loves to learn how to use heavy machinery around her home and in the yard. She loves doing art in collaborative and social situations. She is always up for trying new things and getting her hands dirty even when she is finds that she may not initially be good at it. She welcomes you to do the same. Stephanie is excited to find ways to create and build confidence in who we are becoming as we explore and learn together. She knows that learning about artists and cultures teach us about techniques that we can potentially develop within ourselves. She knows that no matter how imperfect the outcome may be, we can push to find ourselves expressed through our attempts at art.
